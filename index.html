Fla-Flu Derby

Flamengo vs. Fluminense

Est. 1912

A playful name derived from the sides involved, this is Brazil's most fierce rivalry, coming right from the heart of Rio de Janeiro. One game in 1963 saw an incredible 194,000 fans cram to view the spectacle from the stands of the Maracanã.

Derby Di Milano

Internazionale vs. Milan

Est. 1909

Few stadiums in football are a home shared by two teams, but even fewer are are a shared home of two great city rivals. Once a single Milan side, Inter were created following disagreements in 1908, forming what many regard to be the greatest battle in Italian football ever since.
